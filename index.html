Legal & Takedown Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Disclaimer

STREDIO is a personal media library and browser. It does not host, store, upload, cache, or proxy any film or series video files, and its servers never transit media. When you play something, your browser connects directly to a source supplied by a third-party add-on that you chose to install. STREDIO does not control, endorse, or take responsibility for those add-ons or the content they make available. Catalog metadata, cover art, and translations shown here are descriptive information only and are not the copyrighted media itself.

Privacy & logging

STREDIO does not log your IP address or keep server-side request logs. The only data stored against your account is what the service needs to run and sync across your devices — your email, watchlist, continue-watching positions, and add-on preferences — and you can delete your account at any time to remove it.

Takedown / DMCA notices

If you are a copyright owner (or an agent authorised to act on their behalf) and believe a link indexed on STREDIO points to material that infringes your rights, we will act on a valid notice and disable or remove the offending link from our index promptly.

To help us act quickly, please include the following in your notice:

  1. Identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed.
  2. The exact link or page on STREDIO that points to the material.
  3. Your contact details (name, organisation, email, and physical address).
  4. A statement that you have a good-faith belief the use is not authorised by the rights owner, its agent, or the law.
  5. A statement that the information in your notice is accurate, and that you are the rights owner or are authorised to act on their behalf.
  6. Your physical or electronic signature.

Because STREDIO does not host the underlying files, removing a link from our index does not delete the file from the third-party service. To have the file itself removed, the notice must also be sent to the service that actually hosts it.

Contact

Send takedown notices and legal enquiries to:

takedown@stredio.com

We aim to review valid notices within a few business days.
